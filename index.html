Version 1: Dark Purple Pins

Why We Built Where to know

Where to know began with two co-founders brought together by a shared passion for hotels and hospitality. We met as frequent travellers, bonded over loyalty programs and elite upgrades – and quickly realized something was missing in the guest experience cycle.

Across thousands of hotel stays in over 70 countries, we saw the same problem again and again: meaningful guest feedback was often missed, buried, or scattered across platforms.

We knew there had to be a better way:

With roots in global finance at Evercore and enterprise tech at Oracle, we combined our strengths to build something new — a platform that helps hotels truly hear what guests are saying, across reviews, social media, and more.

Where to know turns feedback into insight – and insight into action – so hotels can create the experiences guests actually want.
